ABOUT THE GAME

Heeey Yooou Guuuys is a card matching game. The game board is made up of 8 pairs of matching cards making a total of 16 cards.

Once all 8 pairs have been matched, a player will given a score rating from 1-3 stars and the opportunity to start the game again, to attempt to better their score.

Starting the game:

To start the game either click the PLAY button on the home screen, or click play in the main navigation menu

Matching the cards:

Click a card to flip it over, try and remember the image and flip the next card. Matched cards will remain visible, unmatched cards will flip back over and can be flipped again

Resetting the game:

To reset the cards and start again, click the reset button at the bottom left of the game board:

Game over:

Once all 16 cards are matched, you will presented with your score and a star rating out of 3. Click anywhere to restart and see if you can better your score!

HEEEY YOOOU GUUUYS

The 80's was a strange decade. A time of excess and a time of austerity. Stock market millionnaires and the closure of Britains mining industry and the resulting strikes.

Anyway, this game is not about that. It's a game to remember some of the things that made the 80's great. Things like shoulder pads, the start of the gaming revolution, New Wave make-up, landline telephones and "fat-back" CRT TV's. When you could go the the local shop and buy 100 penny Sweets for a pound and brown corduroy was the pinnacle of interior design.

Hopefully, this card matching game sparks some recessed memory for you, or at least gives you a tiny window into what made the 80's what it was.
