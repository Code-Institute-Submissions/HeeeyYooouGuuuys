ABOUT THE GAME

Heeey Yooou Guuuys is a card matching game. The game board is made up of 8 pairs of matching cards making a total of 16 cards.

Once all 8 pairs have been matched, a player will be given a score rating from 1-3 stars and the opportunity to start the game again, to attempt to better their score.

Starting the game:

To start the game either click the PLAY button on the home screen, or click PLAY in the main navigation menu.

Matching the cards:

Click a card to flip it over, try and remember the image and flip the next card. Matched cards will remain visible, unmatched cards will flip back over and can be flipped again.

Resetting the game:

To reset the cards and start again, click the reset button at the bottom right of the game board.

Winning the game:

Once all 16 cards are matched, you will be presented with your score and a star rating out of 3. Click anywhere to restart and see if you can better your score!
